Character Name: Lirael Nekht-Tet

Personality

Lirael is known for her artistic and creative spirit. She has a natural talent for music and can often be found playing melodic tunes on various instruments. Her presence brings a sense of tranquility to those around her, and she has a close bond with nature.

Backstory

Lirael's connection to nature and music was nurtured by her parents' encouragement of self-expression. She believes that beauty can be found in even the harshest of landscapes. Her artistic endeavors serve as a reminder of the delicate balance between the empire and its environment.
