Character Name: Elthara Nekht-Tet

Personality

Elthara is a quiet and introspective individual. She possesses a keen intellect and a love for learning. She often spends her time lost in books and scrolls, seeking to expand her knowledge. While reserved, she is a compassionate soul and offers insightful advice to her siblings.

Backstory

Elthara's affinity for knowledge and wisdom stems from her desire to understand her people's history and traditions. She sees herself as a guardian of the Sun Elves' cultural heritage and strives to bridge the gap between the past and the present.
